Our Journey

From humble beginnings to leading pharmacy excellence

2026 - Founded

Started by 15 passionate students and the H.O.D dr. Eliani, to address lack of pharmacy representation at KMTC Nyeri.

2027 - First 100 Members

Organize first White Coat Ceremony and secured internship placements for 25 students.

2027 - Best Association Award

Win KMTC National Best Student Association for community health camps before 2028.

2029 - 500+ Members

Become the largest pharmacy association in Central Kenya with digital membership platform.
